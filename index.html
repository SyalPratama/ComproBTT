About Us

Trusted IT Expertise

PT Berkah Teknologi Terdepan present as one of Indonesian IT solutions provider with the vision of becoming an IT company providing solutions according to your business needs. We started with the idea of how to build an enterprise IT efficiently and quickly.

Since it's establishment in 2014, we have developed a close partnership with several hardware and software vendors leading international, among others: HP, IBM, DELL, ERS, Google, Kentico, Meteor. In order to deliver the best solutions for our clients.

We provide one stop solution for your IT-related matters. Whether it's a Data Center Hardware, Human Capital and Employee Tracking Solution, Application Development, Enterprise IT Hardware, Consumer IT hardware and IOT Solution - We handle it all.
